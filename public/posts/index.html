Opt out of internet archive with hugo

If you don’t fancy old versions of your website being archived by The Internet Archive you can use a file called robots.txt to tell its crawler to skip your site. To do this with a hugo website is quite simple. Inside your config.toml add the following; enableRobotsTXT: true Then run your build command; hugo -D
